Nepal: A Land of Natural Beauty

Nepal is a landlocked country located in South Asia, bordered by China and India. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, including the Himalayan mountain range, which is home to the world's highest peak, Mount Everest.

Nepal has a rich cultural heritage, with numerous ethnic and linguistic groups residing in the country. Hinduism and Buddhism are the two major religions, and their influences can be seen in the country's architecture, festivals, and traditions.

Nepal is also famous for its diverse flora and fauna, with numerous national parks and conservation areas protecting the country's natural beauty and wildlife. The country is a popular destination for trekking and mountaineering, attracting thousands of tourists every year.
