The National Anthem of Nepal

The national anthem of Nepal is "Sayaun Thunga Phool Ka". It was composed by Pradeep Kumar Rai and was adopted as the national anthem in 2007.

The anthem reflects the country's rich cultural heritage and natural beauty, with lyrics that describe the unity and pride of the Nepalese people.

Here is the first verse of the national anthem:

"Sayaun Thunga Phool Ka Hami Ekda"
"Nepalko Dhararulo Malikai"
"Maitighar Samjhanai Paryo"
"Hamilai Desh Ko Maan"

Translation: "United we stand, one and all, under a single flag, with our hearts full of pride for our country."
